Graduation Project
SmartTour — AI Travel Planner
A cross-platform mobile app that automates end-to-end trip planning. SmartTour turns the user's inputs — destination, dates, budget, and interests — into a full day-by-day itinerary through an AI pipeline built on Google Gemini 2.5 Flash, integrating the Google Maps & Places APIs, the Booking.com API via RapidAPI for flights and hotels, and Priceline for rental cars.

Built with Flutter on the frontend and Supabase on the backend (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, and Deno Edge Functions running the async AI generation pipeline). A budget engine keeps every plan within the user's limit by combining real flight and hotel prices with AI-estimated activity costs, and a Gemini review step scores each itinerary 0–100 for fit (85–100 across all test cases). SmartTour also works as a social platform — users share, like, comment on, save, and follow trips, and get help from a Gemini-powered chat assistant.
